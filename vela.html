Vela Project

What is Vela?

Vela is a responive web app designed to keep it's users safe by giving them accurate current and future weather forecasts. Vela is a designed for individuals who like to spend their time outside and on the water.

My Role

I am the lead UX Designer and UX Reseacher.

Duration

7 Months

Problem

People who spend their time on the water need an app that shows accurate weather forecasts because they have trouble remaining safe on unplanned days.

User Personas

To make a user persona one must be able to understand their users. I conducted research and host interviews with three participants. From their insights I was able to make these personas.
